IWoAT Summer School 2024: Motivic Stable Homotopy Theory

    Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences, Fudan University
  • Shanghai, China
  • June 23–27, 2024

About

IWoAT Summer School 2024 is a five-day event which will take place at Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences of Fudan University in Shanghai, China from Sunday, June 23 to Thursday, June 27, 2024. The topic of the summer school is “motivic stable homotopy theory.”
