About

IWoAT Summer School 2026: Stable homotopy theory will take place at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS) of Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing, China from Monday, August 10 to Friday, August 14, 2026. The main topic of the summer school is stable homotopy theory, the modern theoretical foundation of algebraic topology.

The Scientific Committee is chaired by Prof. Andrew J. Blumberg (Columbia University). The summer school is aimed at advanced undergraduates, graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and researchers interested in algebraic topology and stable homotopy theory.
