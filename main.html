International Workshop on Algebraic Topology

About

The International Workshop on Algebraic Topology (IWoAT) is a series of events focusing on topics in algebraic topology.

The IWoAT conference series was initiated at Southern University of Science and Technology in 2018, and will see its fifth year this year. Before Covid, in the summer of 2019, our 5-day summer school and 3-day conference at Shanghai Center for Mathematical Sciences attracted more than 130 participants from around the world. During Covid, we held a hybrid summer school in 2021 on equivariant stable homotopy theory, and an online summer school in 2022 on higher algebra and its applications.

This year, we will host a one-week conference (in-person) in July and a summer school in August.

We plan to continue strengthening and expanding the connections within the community of broader algebraic topology and related fields.
