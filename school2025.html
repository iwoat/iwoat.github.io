IWoAT Summer School 2025: Computations of Stable Homotopy Groups

    Institute for Theoretical Sciences, Westlake University
  • Shanghai, China
  • July 1–5, 2025

About

IWoAT Summer School 2025 is a five-day event which will take place at Institute for Theoretical Sciences of Westlake University in Hangzhou, China from Tuesday, July 1 to Saturday, July 5, 2025. The topic of the summer school is “Computations of Stable Homotopy Groups.”
