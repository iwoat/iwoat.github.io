About

The International Workshop on Algebraic Topology 2025 is a five-day event which will take place at School of Mathematical Sciences of Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China from Monday, July 7 to Friday, July 11, 2025.

The week before this conference, we will also be hosting another event. For more details, please visit IWoAT Summer School 2025.
